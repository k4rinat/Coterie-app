Conversion lever
Stage 03 Demo — The demo call is the highest-converting touchpoint. Every brand that attends a demo converts at a significantly higher rate than those who don't.
Primary friction
Stage 06 Introduce — Brands expect instant access. The mediated model requires clear upfront explanation to prevent drop-off.
Retention lever
Stage 07 Hire — A successful first hire within 30 days is the strongest predictor of brand renewal and repeat use.
